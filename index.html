Fight to   50

How To Play

1. Both players will be given the same random number between 0 and 1000, and can only add, subtract, multiply, or divide by numbers from 0 to 9 (multiplication by 0 is not allowed).

2. Players can only use one arithmetic operation on their respective numbers per turn.

3. If players perform multiply/divide in the current turn, then they can only use add/subtract in their next turn and vice versa.

4. Both players will be given a total of 2 minutes each. The player with no time left will lose.

5. The player who takes their number to 50 first wins.
